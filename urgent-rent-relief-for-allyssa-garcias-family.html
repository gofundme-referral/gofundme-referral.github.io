Ari Rivera is organizing this fundraiser.
  • Created 3 days ago
  • Babies, Kids & Family
I read an article about Allyssa Garcia's struggle to pay rent and how she faces homelessness because of reduced hours at her job and no rent relief. I reached out the the writer of the article, Susan Dunlap, who put me in contact with Allyssa. I was hoping someone had already started a gofundme for Allyssa where I could pitch in. No one had so I thought I would try to raise money for her. The story made me realize how lucky I was to have no problem paying my own rent and how terrible the prospect of homelessness would be during this pandemic.
